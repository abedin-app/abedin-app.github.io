Features of Abedin application

Record daily activity

You can record the things you do every day and get reports from them

Keeping memories

You and each of your friends can keep memories and important events of that day for each day

Voice memo

You can make voice notes for the coming days

Wake up time recording

You can record the time you sleep and wake up every day and see the report

Record events

You can record the important events that happened to you during the day
